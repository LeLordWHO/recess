I'm writing a book.

I can prove it. I have a word document called "book". And another called "book ideas". These are all I need to get started.

I'm in a book club. We read modern fiction. Modern fiction has reminded me that anyone can write a book.

A good book should have three things. A beginning. A middle. And a twist. The twist is the most important part. Name one good book without a twist. You can't.

My book has a big twist. But so far the biggest twist is that I'm writing a book at all.

When I'm done writing my book I'll have it printed in the usual sizes. Large. Medium. And bathroom book.

A bathroom book is one you read while you're in there. Ideally a bathroom book should be light reading. The idea is that you shouldn't be in there too long. I have a different idea. My book will keep you on the edge of your seat. My book will keep you backed up.

My favourite size of book is medium. My favourite genre of book is war.

The first book I read I don't remember. The first book I tried to write was The Bible. I took out an empty notebook and began copying out page one of Genesis. I thought I'd like to have a copy in my own handwriting. A seven year old's handwriting. I didn't get very far as The Bible is a size large.

It was hard to write The Bible. I hope my next book will be easier.
