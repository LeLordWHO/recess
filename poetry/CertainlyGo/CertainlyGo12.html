in the room of words
here is that line b/w,
resembling
the line from which our words emerge;
shine breaks thru—
shadows on the snow
cast by pine trees

the same things in the same order
but underneath: we have deprived
them of their origins,
they have come to need us now—

looked a lot like water—
lights came shining thru the trees—
the river's black mirror—
my name, not spoken but shouted
from the jersey side of the delaware—

grievings, cravings,
subtle curves of miserable;
these pills the doctor told me to—
these memories make me nonfunctional—
